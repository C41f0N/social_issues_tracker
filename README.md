# Idraak

A social media app exclusively to monitor and highlight social issues.

## About
This project explores the idea of a social media platform built specifically to highlight social issues and encourage community participation. Instead of focusing on trends, influencers, or entertainment, the app centers real-world concerns that often get lost or overlooked on traditional platforms.

Users can share  social issues affecting their communities, contribute firsthand experiences, and help bring attention to causes that matter to them. Community involvement plays a key role, people can engage with posts, add context, and collectively surface the most important and urgent issues.

The goal of this project is to reimagine social media as a space for awareness, empathy, and action, showing how digital platforms can support social responsibility rather than just passive scrolling.
